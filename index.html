Fiction

If you like escaping the real world and enter the worlds of some fictious characters, go through their character-building, blindsiding plot-twists and an emotional roller-coaster, then this genre is the one for you.

Non-Fiction

If you like diving deep into the real world philosophies, truths, spiritualism and history, then non-fiction is the one for you. You can also get inspired by life stories of great leaders.

Both

If you don't have a specific type yet, or you're a beginner, or you like reading all genres of books, then you can pick out 5-star reads of both fiction and non-fiction to help you in the process of reading.
